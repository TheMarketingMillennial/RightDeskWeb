Why Documentation Matters

When something goes wrong at work — whether it's harassment, retaliation, or unfair treatment — the most important thing you can do is document everything, immediately. Memories fade. Witnesses move on. Without written records, it becomes your word against theirs.

These guides walk you through practical, proven methods for building a paper trail that protects you — before you need a lawyer.
