How It Works

From Incident to Report
in 3 Simple Steps

No legal knowledge required. RightDesk guides you through the entire documentation process.

1
Log the Incident

Record what happened — who was involved, when, where, and how it affected you. Use voice or type it out.

2
Build Your Case

Add follow-up incidents, attach notes, and watch your timeline build. RightDesk detects patterns automatically.

3
Generate Your Report

Choose your report type — HR, harassment, discrimination, retaliation, and more. AI writes a professional narrative instantly.

4
Take Action

Export as PDF, share with an employment attorney, or submit to HR. Your documentation is ready when you are.

Our Story

Built From Personal Experience

"RightDesk Reports: Empowering employees, protecting rights. Because your voice deserves to be heard."

RightDesk Reports was born from firsthand experience with toxic workplaces and deceptive management. After navigating unfair treatment with no proper tools to document or defend against it, the founder set out to create the resource they wished they'd had — a private, powerful, professional tool that puts employees on equal footing.

Today, RightDesk is a lifeline for employees dealing with discrimination, harassment, retaliation, and workplace inequity. We believe every worker deserves the tools to tell their story — and be taken seriously.
